JEE 6HV Automatic Concrete Brick Making Machine

Industrial Excellence in Brick Manufacturing

The JEE 6HV automatic concrete brick making machine stands as a symbol of innovation and efficiency in industrial brick manufacturing. Powered by a robust 22 HP engine and designed for seamless integration into industrial processes, this machine sets new standards for productivity, precision, and performance.

The JEE 6HV boasts fully automatic operation, leveraging cutting-edge technology to streamline the brick manufacturing process. With minimal manual intervention required, this machine delivers consistent results with maximum efficiency.

Equipped with a powerful 22 HP motor and designed to operate at a frequency of 50 Hz and a voltage of 440 V 3PH, the JEE 6HV ensures reliable performance and stability, even in demanding industrial environments.

With a production capacity of 1000 bricks per day, the JEE 6HV offers unparalleled output, making it ideal for industrial-scale brick manufacturing operations. Its high efficiency and productivity help industrial facilities meet production targets and timelines with ease.

Featuring a tolerance of just 0.5, the JEE 6HV ensures precise brick manufacturing, resulting in uniformity in size, shape, and quality across every batch. This precision engineering minimizes wastage and maximizes the value of raw materials.

Designed for industrial applications, the JEE 6HV is well-suited for a wide range of construction projects, from large-scale infrastructure developments to commercial complexes and residential communities. Its versatility makes it a valuable asset for industrial facilities seeking to expand their capabilities in brick manufacturing.

Producing bricks with dimensions of 230 x 110 x 75 mm, the JEE 6HV adheres to industry-standard sizes while offering flexibility in design and construction. These standardized brick dimensions ensure compatibility with a wide range of building projects and applications.
