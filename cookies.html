Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2024

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by enabling us to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Basic functionality

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

  • Page views
  • User behavior
  • Traffic sources

Preference Cookies

These cookies enable the website to remember information that changes the way the website behaves or looks.

  • Language preferences
  • Region settings
  • Custom preferences

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your online activity to help advertisers deliver more relevant advertising.

  • Ad targeting
  • Social media integration
  • Remarketing

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data

Third-Party Cookies

We use services from third parties that may also set cookies on your device. These include:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Social media platforms for sharing features
  • Payment processors for secure transactions

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page.
