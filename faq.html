General Questions

Jee Engineers is a leading manufacturer of fly ash brick making machines and concrete block making machines. We specialize in providing high-quality, eco-friendly construction equipment and solutions.

We are located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. Our manufacturing facility is equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and technology.

Fly ash bricks are eco-friendly bricks made from fly ash, a waste product from coal combustion. They are preferred for their durability, strength, and environmentally friendly properties.

Was this helpful?

Fly ash bricks offer advantages such as reduced water absorption, higher compressive strength, and better insulation properties compared to traditional clay bricks.

Yes, fly ash bricks are cost-effective due to their reduced production costs, longer lifespan, and energy-efficient properties, making them an excellent choice for builders and contractors.
