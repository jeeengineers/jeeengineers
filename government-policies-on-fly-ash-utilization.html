Understanding Government Policies on Fly Ash Utilization

India has implemented comprehensive policies to promote the effective utilization of fly ash, addressing environmental concerns while creating economic opportunities. This guide explores key regulations, compliance requirements, and incentives for businesses in the construction sector.

Key Government Policies on Fly Ash Utilization

India â€“ Leading the Way in Fly Ash Regulations

India is the largest producer of fly ash (~250 million tons/year). Key policies include:

Fly Ash Notification (2021)
  • Mandates 100% utilization of fly ash by coal plants
  • Requires fly ash bricks within 300 km of thermal power plants
PMAY

Subsidies for affordable housing using fly ash bricks

GST Reduction

Fly ash bricks taxed at 5% GST vs. 12-18% for clay bricks
